IMPORTANT NOTE:

As of 2023, I'm actually basically on a (likely permanent) hiatus from making stuff for Second Life.

It was fun, I learned a lot of my basics with modeling and texturing while doing it; it isn't going to pay my bills. I've been shifting my focus to game development for a while, and making stuff for SL takes way too much time and effort that I could be using for furthering a career.

That said, I'm not closing my stores, and I'm keeping my inworld space up as long as I can, so if you'd like to visit the store, feel free. Just note my customer service will likely be much slower than it used to be. :')
